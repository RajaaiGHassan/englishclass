Mass tourism

Mass tourism has become, one of the most significant phenomena of the modern era, bringing millions of visitors to popular destinations each year. While this industry generates significant economic benefits for local communities, it also presents serious challenges. Overcrowding, rising living costs, and environmental degradation are just a few issues cities face due to the influx of visitors. Authorities are increasingly implementing regulations such as tourist caps, entry fees, and sustainable tourism policies to balance the benefits and drawbacks of mass travel.
